1,3-Butadiene — Known Excited-State Dynamics

Photoexcitation

  • Ground-state s-trans conformer; UV excitation populates the bright $S_2$ ($\pi\pi^*$) state
  • Ultrafast internal conversion $S_2 \to S_1$, typically tens of fs

The $S_1$ Conical Intersection

  • $S_1$ evolves toward a twisted, pyramidalized geometry at one terminal carbon — the conical intersection to $S_0$
  • Passage through the CI returns population to the ground state on a sub-200 fs timescale

Branching at the CI

  • Re-forms s-trans, isomerises to the other double-bond rotamer, or (minor channel) ring-closes to cyclobutene
  • Relative branching between simple torsional isomerisation and deeper pyramidalization pathways is the open question this measurement probes

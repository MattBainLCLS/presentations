Generating Short UV Pulses

Harmonic Generation

  • Crystal-based — straightforward route to 20–30 fs
  • Confined to discrete wavelengths (harmonics of the fundamental)

(N)OPA

  • Continuously tunable output wavelength
  • Trade-off between pulse duration and pulse energy
  • Sub-20 fs achievable, but needs complex optical setups

Alternate Route: HCF Solitons

  • Soliton dynamics in a gas-filled hollow-core fibre
  • Tunable, sub-10 fs, simple single-fibre setup — the route this talk explores

Fiber Types

Guidance, Briefly

  • Solid core: light in glass, guided by TIR — high dispersion/nonlinearity
  • Hollow core: light in gas, guided by microstructure — ~1000× lower dispersion/nonlinearity

Fission Must Beat Loss

  • The soliton must self-compress and fission before the pulse is attenuated
  • Requires $L_\text{fiss} \ll L_\text{loss}$: the fission length must sit well within the loss length
  • Hollow core's low loss gives a long $L_\text{loss}$ — the room the dynamics need to develop

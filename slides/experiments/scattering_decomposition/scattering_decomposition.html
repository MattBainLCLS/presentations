Scattering Pattern Decomposition

  • $\Delta S$ averaged over 400–800 fs, split into two structural classes: trans-like (within bound) and isomerised (exceeded bound)
  • The two classes give distinct $Q$-space signatures — notably around the 2.6 Å⁻¹ shoulder and the 4.5 Å⁻¹ peak
  • The missing 2.5–3.5 Å⁻¹ feature reappears strongly in the isomerised-only component — it is washed out once averaged into the full population
  • Suggests the simulations underestimate branching into the isomerisation pathway relative to pyramidalisation around the terminal carbon
